About RefiMath

Free, unbiased mortgage tools for homeowners making big financial decisions.

What We Do

RefiMath builds simple, honest calculators that help homeowners understand whether refinancing their mortgage makes financial sense. We strip away the complexity and give you one number that matters: your break-even point — the exact month your savings exceed your closing costs.

Our tools are free, require no sign-up, and are not affiliated with any lender. We have no incentive to push you toward refinancing if it doesn't make sense for your situation.

Why We Built This

Most online mortgage calculators are either buried inside a lender's website (biased toward their products), cluttered with ads that obscure the results, or technically correct but impossible to understand.

We built RefiMath because homeowners deserve a clean, fast, honest tool — one that gives a clear answer and explains the math behind it.

Accuracy & Disclaimer

Our calculators use standard mortgage amortization formulas and are designed for educational and planning purposes. Results are estimates only and do not constitute financial advice. Closing costs, tax implications, and lender-specific terms vary — always consult a licensed mortgage professional before making a refinancing decision.

We update our tools regularly to reflect current market conditions. Rate references on this site come from publicly available sources including Bankrate, Freddie Mac, and the Federal Reserve.
